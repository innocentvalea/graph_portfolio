About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8-week live online certification that teaches no-code AI techniques to automate documents, data analysis, and research workflows.

Key Modules

1. Content & Websites

LLM fundamentals, no-code sites, AI presentations.

2. Automated Data Analysis

Code Interpreter, Julius, Windsurf dashboards.

3. Research & Text Analysis

Literature search, summarisation, sentiment analysis.

4. Custom GPTs & Chatbots

RAG workflows, API integrations, Botpress.

5. AI Task Automation

Lindy, Make.com, GSheets and Gmail automations.

6. Communication & Outreach

Ideogram visuals, marketing flows, video production.
